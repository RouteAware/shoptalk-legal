Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you register and use ShopTalk, we collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Display name, email address, phone number, date of birth
  • Profile Information: Bio, workplace affiliation, profile photo
  • Content You Create: Posts, comments, marketplace listings, event information
  • Communication Data: Feedback submissions, support requests
  • Contact Preferences: Selected contact methods (phone, text, email) for marketplace/events

Location Information

  • Precise Location: We collect your GPS coordinates when you use location-based features
  • Geofencing: Location data is used to verify you're within your workplace radius (required for posting)
  • Background Location: We do NOT collect location data when the app is not in use
  • Location Permissions: You must grant location access to use core features

How We Use Location Data:

  • Verify you're within workplace boundaries before allowing posts
  • Show you content from your workplace community
  • Filter events and marketplace items by proximity

Photos and Camera

  • Photo Library Access: To upload images to posts, marketplace listings, and events
  • Camera Access: To take photos directly within the app
  • Image Storage: Photos are stored on our servers and associated with your content

Automatically Collected Information

When you use ShopTalk, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, crash logs
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics, error logs

Analytics and Tracking

  • Anonymous Analytics: We collect anonymized usage data to improve the app
  • Advertising Data (Future): When ads are implemented, we will collect advertising identifiers for targeted advertising
  • Tracking Permissions: We request your permission under Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework

How We Use Your Information

Provide and Maintain the App

  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable posting, commenting, and content creation
  • Facilitate marketplace transactions (peer-to-peer communication)
  • Organize and display workplace events
  • Verify you're within workplace boundaries

Communication

  • Send you notifications about comments, messages, and activity
  • Respond to your feedback and support requests
  • Send important updates about the app or policy changes

Safety and Security

  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or policy violations
  • Investigate and address reported content
  • Comply with legal obligations

Analytics and Improvement

  • Understand how users interact with the app
  • Identify bugs and technical issues
  • Improve app features and user experience
  • Develop new features

How We Share Your Information

Information Shared Publicly

  • Posts and Comments: Visible to all users in your workplace
  • Anonymous Posts: Posted without your name, but we maintain internal records
  • Marketplace Listings: Your selected contact methods are visible to interested buyers
  • Events: Your selected contact methods are visible to attendees
  • Profile Information: Display name and bio are visible to other users

Third-Party Service Providers

We share your information with trusted third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and user authentication
  • Expo: App infrastructure and push notifications
  • Google Maps: Location services and mapping
  • Resend (Future): Email delivery service
  • Google AdMob (Future): Advertising platform

Data Processing Agreements: All third-party providers are contractually required to protect your data.

Legal and Safety Disclosures

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or security threats
  • Cooperate with law enforcement

Employer Access (Limited Circumstances)

Important: ShopTalk is NOT an official employer platform.

However, we may disclose information to employers if:

  • Legal obligation requires it
  • Serious policy violations occur (illegal activity, threats, harassment)
  • We receive a valid legal request from the employer

We will not proactively share your activity with employers for routine monitoring.

Your Data Rights

Access Your Data

You have the right to request:

  • A copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Information about how we use your data
  • Details about third parties we share data with

How to Request: Email info@alexandermhughes.com with subject "Data Access Request"

Delete Your Data

You have the right to request deletion of your account and personal data:

  • In-App Deletion: Profile → Settings → Delete My Account
  • Email Request: info@alexandermhughes.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"

What Gets Deleted:

  • Your account and profile information
  • Your posts and comments (including anonymous ones)
  • Your marketplace listings and events
  • Your uploaded photos and content
  • Your activity logs and preferences

What May Be Retained:

  • Data required by law (transaction records, legal holds)
  • Anonymized analytics data
  • Backup copies (deleted within 90 days)

Export Your Data

You have the right to receive a copy of your data in a portable format:

  • In-App Export: Profile → Settings → Download My Data
  • Email Request: info@alexandermhughes.com with subject "Data Export Request"

California Residents (CCPA)

If you're a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Know: What personal data we collect, use, and share
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of sale of personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

Important: We do NOT sell your personal information.

European Residents (GDPR)

If you're in the European Union, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing and token-based auth
  • Access Controls: Role-based access to databases and systems
  • Row-Level Security: Database policies restrict data access

Important Limitations

No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

You are responsible for:

  • Keeping your password secure
  • Not sharing your account credentials
  • Logging out on shared devices
