ShopTalk Community Guidelines & Terms of Service

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

Version: 1.0

Welcome to ShopTalk

ShopTalk is a workplace community platform designed to connect coworkers, facilitate collaboration, and build a positive workplace culture. These Community Guidelines and Terms of Service help ensure ShopTalk remains a safe, respectful, and productive space for everyone.

By using ShopTalk, you agree to follow these guidelines and our Terms of Service.

Core Principles

1. Respect Everyone

  • Treat all users with dignity and respect
  • Value diverse perspectives and backgrounds
  • Disagree constructively and professionally
  • Remember: your coworkers will see what you post

2. Keep It Professional

  • This is a workplace app - maintain professional standards
  • Consider how your posts reflect on you and your workplace
  • Use appropriate language and tone
  • Think before you post

3. Build Community

  • Contribute positively to discussions
  • Support and encourage coworkers
  • Share helpful information and resources
  • Organize events that bring people together

4. Stay Safe

  • Protect your personal information
  • Report suspicious or harmful behavior
  • Don't share sensitive workplace information
  • Verify before you trust

What's Allowed

Encouraged Content

  • Workplace Events: Team lunches, happy hours, community gatherings
  • Marketplace Items: Buy/sell items to coworkers safely
  • General Discussion: Work-life topics, local recommendations, hobbies
  • Support and Advice: Professional development, workplace questions
  • Anonymous Feedback: Constructive feedback about workplace issues
  • Community Building: Meetups, clubs, shared interests

What's Not Allowed

1. Harassment and Bullying

You may not:

  • Harass, threaten, or intimidate other users
  • Engage in persistent unwanted contact
  • Cyberbully or target individuals
  • Share content intended to humiliate others
  • Organize harassment campaigns
Examples of harassment:
• Repeated hostile messages to someone who asked you to stop
• Posting rumors or lies to damage someone's reputation
• Sharing embarrassing photos of coworkers without consent
• Creating posts targeting a specific person negatively

2. Hate Speech and Discrimination

You may not post content that:

  • Promotes violence or hatred based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, age, disability, or veteran status
  • Uses slurs or derogatory language
  • Advocates for discrimination
  • Promotes hate groups or ideologies

3. Illegal Activity

You may not:

  • Sell or promote illegal goods or services
  • Facilitate illegal drug use
  • Share pirated or stolen content
  • Promote illegal activities
  • Violate intellectual property rights
  • Engage in fraud or scams

4. Adult and Sexual Content

You may not post:

  • Pornography or sexually explicit content
  • Nudity or sexual imagery
  • Sexual solicitation or propositions
  • Content sexualizing minors (ZERO tolerance)

5. Violence and Threats

You may not:

  • Threaten violence against anyone
  • Encourage self-harm or suicide
  • Glorify or promote violence
  • Share graphic violent content
  • Organize dangerous activities

6. Misinformation and Scams

You may not:

  • Deliberately spread false information
  • Impersonate others
  • Run scams or fraudulent schemes
  • Mislead about product condition or pricing
  • Create fake accounts or manipulate systems

7. Spam and Commercial Abuse

You may not:

  • Spam repetitive content
  • Post excessive promotional content
  • Use bots or automation
  • Manipulate engagement (fake likes, comments)
  • Cross-post the same content repeatedly

8. Privacy Violations

You may not:

  • Share others' private information (doxxing)
  • Post private conversations without consent
  • Share sensitive workplace information
  • Violate confidentiality agreements
  • Post photos of people without their consent

Anonymous Posting Guidelines

Anonymous posting is a privilege, not a right. It allows honest feedback but must not be abused.

Allowed Anonymous Posts

  • Constructive workplace feedback
  • Reporting safety or ethics concerns
  • Discussing sensitive personal topics
  • Asking questions you're uncomfortable asking publicly

NOT Allowed Anonymous Posts

  • Harassment or personal attacks
  • Spreading false information about individuals
  • Evading bans or rules
  • Anything that would violate guidelines if posted publicly

Important: Anonymous posts are not truly anonymous to ShopTalk administrators. We maintain records and will reveal identities if legal obligations require it or serious policy violations occur.

Marketplace Guidelines

Seller Responsibilities

  • Accurately describe items and condition
  • Price items fairly
  • Respond to inquiries promptly
  • Mark items as sold when no longer available
  • Arrange safe, public meetups for transactions

Buyer Responsibilities

  • Communicate respectfully with sellers
  • Show up for arranged meetings
  • Inspect items before purchasing
  • Don't pressure or harass sellers

Disputes: ShopTalk does not mediate buyer/seller disputes. All transactions are peer-to-peer at your own risk.

Event Guidelines

Event Organizer Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate event information
  • Update or cancel events if plans change
  • Cancel specific recurring event dates if needed
  • Respond to attendee questions
  • Ensure events comply with workplace policies

Prohibited Events

  • Events promoting illegal activity
  • Events violating workplace policies
  • Discriminatory or exclusive events (based on protected classes)
  • Events that create safety risks

Reporting Violations

How to Report

In-App Reporting:

  1. Tap the "..." menu on any post, comment, listing, or event
  2. Select "Report"
  3. Choose violation category
  4. Provide details (optional but helpful)

Email Reporting:

Email: info@alexandermhughes.com

Subject: "Content Report - ShopTalk"

Include: Screenshot, link, or description of violation

What We Do With Reports

  1. Review: We review all reports (typically within 24-48 hours)
  2. Investigate: We gather context and examine the content
  3. Decide: We determine if guidelines were violated
  4. Act: We take appropriate enforcement action
  5. Follow-Up: We notify the reporter and the violator

Enforcement and Consequences

Enforcement Actions

1. Warning

  • First-time minor violations
  • Ambiguous situations
  • Educational opportunity

2. Content Removal

  • Delete violating posts, comments, listings, or events
  • May be with or without warning

3. Temporary Suspension

  • Block account access for 24 hours to 30 days
  • For repeated or serious violations
  • Account remains but cannot post or interact

4. Permanent Ban

  • Complete account deletion
  • All content removed
  • Cannot create new accounts
  • Reserved for severe violations, repeated violations, or ban evasion

5. Legal Action

  • Report to law enforcement if content is illegal
  • Comply with legal requests
  • Cooperate with investigations

Appeals Process

If you believe enforcement action was wrong:

  1. Email Appeal: info@alexandermhughes.com
  2. Subject Line: "ShopTalk Appeal - [Your Username]"
  3. Provide:
    • Your username and email
    • What action was taken
    • Why you believe it was wrong
    • Any relevant context

Review Time: We review appeals within 7 business days.

Our decision on appeals is final.

Age Requirement

You must be 18 years or older to use ShopTalk.

  • Age verification during signup
  • Accounts blocked if under 18
  • COPPA compliance (no children under 13)

Workplace Considerations

Important Disclaimer: ShopTalk is NOT an official employer platform and is not endorsed by or affiliated with your employer.

  • Your employer may have policies about workplace apps - review them
  • Content you post may be discoverable by coworkers and management
  • Use discretion when posting workplace-related content
  • We may disclose information to employers if legally required

Updates to These Guidelines

We may update these Community Guidelines and Terms of Service to address new issues or improve clarity.

When we update:

  • Version number will change
  • "Last Updated" date will change
  • We may notify you in-app
  • Continued use means you accept changes

Summary

Do:

  • Be respectful and professional
  • Use ShopTalk to build community
  • Report violations
  • Protect your privacy
  • Follow marketplace and event etiquette

Don't:

  • Harass, bully, or threaten others
  • Post illegal, sexual, or violent content
  • Spam or scam
  • Share private information
  • Abuse anonymous posting

Remember:

  • Your coworkers can see what you post
  • Anonymous doesn't mean consequence-free
  • We take violations seriously
  • When in doubt, don't post it

Contact
